Mission

Impervious is building technology to help bring freedom to the internet.

Projects

Past experiments

Beacon - A DNSSEC enabled browser with a P2P trust anchor.

Fingertip - A decentralized resolver.

Bitcoin Focus

At Impervious, we believe Bitcoin is the foundation for a more free and open internet. Our development efforts are focused exclusively on Bitcoin and Bitcoin-adjacent technologies that enhance privacy, security, and self-sovereignty.
